Some of the amazing features Habitify are listed below:

Track

Track your progress of an activity multiple times a day, week, month. See how well you managed your progrss with multiple bars, graphical visualization, percentage and numbers. We provide you with multiple options and sub-options for your habit and goals.

Streak

Maintain your streak by tracking your habit everyday. We recommend you to do atleast 100 days for amazing results. Try built-in options like 100 Days of Code, 100 Days of Workout, etc. Also you will be awarded unique gifts and amazing rewards on completion.

Sync

Sync your progress with google fit to track your calories and distance travelled. We also provide this app to sync it with multiple applications for your ease. We also provide your progress to be synced with twitter and update your daily results automatically.

Some General tips apart from using this app:

  • Maintain at-most 5-8 tracks at a time. More no. of tracking will be tough for you to maintain.
  • Use this app daily to track your real-time progress.
  • Start Slow. And try to improve a little every day.
  • Don't Panic if you missed a day. But make sure to not miss again.
  • Don't pressurize yourself too much.
  • You're being productive. So try to spend not more than 10 minutes on this app too.
  • It's good to be unproductive for a while.
